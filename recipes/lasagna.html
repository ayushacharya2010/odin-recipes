Ingredients

  • Meat
    • Sweet Italian sausage
    • Lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic - cooked with the meat to add flavor
    • One onion
    • Two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products
    • Can of crushed tomatoes
    • Two cans of tomato sauce
    • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar - two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
    • Fresh parsley
    • Dried basil leaves
    • Salt
    • Italian seasoning
    • Fennel seeds
    • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles - store bought or homemade
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers once more, then top with remaining Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake -
    50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees fahrenheit. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for about 15 minutes before serving
